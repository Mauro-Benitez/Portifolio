Get to know me!

I'm a Software Support and Backend-focused professional, working on the investigation and resolution of production issues involving APIs, databases, and backend systems.

My role involves deep technical analysis such as reviewing logs, debugging SQL procedures and scripts, identifying data inconsistencies, and troubleshooting integration problems between services. I also participate in controlled system changes (GMUD) to ensure stability and reliability in production environments.

Beyond my daily work, I build personal projects focused on backend development, APIs, and cloud technologies, aiming to strengthen my skills in building scalable and reliable systems.

I'm particularly interested in Backend Engineering, Cloud Architecture, IA, and Distributed Systems.
